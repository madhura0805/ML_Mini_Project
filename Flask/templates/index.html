About

Agriculture sustains life, but plant diseases can severely affect productivity and farmer income. Early and accurate detection helps prevent large-scale crop loss and reduces unnecessary pesticide use. Plant-AI leverages machine learning to identify diseases directly from leaf images, making detection faster, easier, and more accessible for farmers everywhere.
